Site Plan for Ojo de Agua Optics

This document outlines the planned structure and content for the Ojo de Agua Optics website.

Site Name

Ojo de Agua Optics

This name reflects the personal branding of my father's longstanding expertise and reputation in the optician field.

Optional domain availability: ojodeagua.com

Site Purpose

The Ojo de Agua Optics site will serve as both a digital storefront and an informational resource to help clients understand the services and products offered.

Scenarios

What types of glasses are best for children or people with active lifestyles?

How can I book an eye examination appointment online?

Color Schema

The menu and other items will use #029FC6

The titles will use this color #020b2e

The Background color #eefbfc

Typography

All the titles and buttons will use the Amaranth font

All the paragraphs will use the Carlito font
